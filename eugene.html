  • Population: As of 2023, the population of Eugene is estimated to be 175,685 people, making it the third-largest city in Oregon and the 151st largest city in the United States.

  • Year incorporated: Eugene was incorporated in 1862 and was named after its founder, Eugene Franklin Skinner.

  • Region of the state: Eugene is located in western Oregon, in the Willamette Valley region. It is situated on the banks of the Willamette River, and is adjacent to the city of Springfield to the east.

  • City classification: Eugene is classified as an urban city, with a mix of residential, commercial, and industrial areas.

  • Average income level, the median household income in Eugene is $55,418, which is slightly lower than the Oregon state median of $68,319. However, the cost of living in Eugene is lower than the national average, which can offset the lower income levels.
