  • Portland is the largest city in Oregon, located in Multnomah County. The median household income in Portland is $76,410, which is slightly higher than the state average.

  • Portland has a population of approximately 652,000 people and Portland is considered an urban city with a mix of residential, commercial, and industrial areas.

  • Portland was incorporated in 1851 and is part of the Portland Metropolitan Area in the northwest region of the state.
