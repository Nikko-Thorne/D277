  • The capital city of Oregon is Salem, which is located in Marion County and has a population of about 173,000.

  • The city, which is a part of the Willamette Valley region, was founded in 1857 and is categorized as an urban location since it has a mix of residential and commercial districts.

  • The median household income in Salem is somewhat lower at $62,232. under the $68,319 state average.
