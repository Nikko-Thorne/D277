  • Salem, which is situated in Marion County, serves as the capital city of Oregon and is home to an estimated 173,000 individuals.

  • The city, which is part of the Willamette Valley region, was established in 1857 and features a blend of residential and commercial areas, thus classified as an urban locale.

  • At $62,232, the median household income in Salem is somewhat below the state's average of $68,319.
